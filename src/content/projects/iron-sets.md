# IronSets

**IronSets** is a streamlined workout tracker built to replace complex fitness apps with a focus on speed and data integrity.

## ⏱️ Development Journey

This project was conceived, designed, and developed within a single month. The goal was to ship a high-quality, functional MVP that integrates deeply with the Apple ecosystem.

*   **Timeline**: 1 Month Sprint
*   **Submission Date**: February 7, 2026
*   **Status**: Available on the App Store.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

*   **Platform**: iOS & watchOS
*   **Framework**: SwiftUI
*   **Health Integration**: Full HealthKit support for syncing workouts and biometric data.
